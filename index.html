Leisure & Bonding

Beyond the corporate tours and seminars, we also enjoyed much-needed breathers and meaningful moments in Tagaytay and Baguio:

Overview

From June 8 to 15, 2025, our CS141 class embarked on a week-long industry immersion across various leading tech companies and scenic locations in the Philippines. This blog captures the essence of our journey — from seminars and facility tours to moments of rest and bonding.

Personal Reflections

This trip was more than just an academic requirement — it was a chance to grow both personally and professionally. Seeing real companies and talking to industry professionals made everything we’ve been studying feel more real. I gained clarity on the path I want to take in the tech world, and I’ve come to appreciate the importance of collaboration, communication, and adaptability.

Tagaytay and Baguio reminded me that taking a step back is also part of growth. Whether it was sharing stories on the bus, laughing over food, or simply admiring the views, those little moments added something special to the experience. This journey gave me new perspectives, stronger friendships, and motivation to keep moving forward.
